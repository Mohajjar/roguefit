Frequently Asked Questions

What is online coaching? +

Online coaching provides personalized fitness and nutrition guidance from a certified coach, delivered remotely through digital platforms. This includes custom workout plans, meal guidance, and virtual check-ins.

How are workout plans customized? +

Upon enrollment, you'll complete an in-depth assessment of your fitness level, goals, medical history, and available equipment. Our coaches then design a plan unique to your needs and preferences.

Can I switch plans later? +

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes typically take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What if I have an injury? +

Please inform your coach of any existing injuries. Plans will be modified to ensure your safety and promote recovery. We recommend consulting a medical professional for severe injuries.
